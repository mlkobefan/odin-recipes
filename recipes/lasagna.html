Lasagna Recipe

Description

Who doesn't love layers of pasta?

This recipe is easy, quick, and feeds many people.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crumbs
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Eggs, Whisked
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven by setting to broil
  2. Butterfly the chicken breast and pound with meat tenderizer until thin
  3. Cover chicken in flour, then egg mixture, then bread crumbs.
  4. Fry chicken in vegetable oil over medium heat
  5. Place on baking tray and cover in tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese
  6. Bake until golden brown
  7. Top with parsley
